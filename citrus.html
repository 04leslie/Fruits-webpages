Citrus Fruits

What are citrus fruits?

Citrus fruits are edible fruits that belong to the genus Citrus, and family Rutaceae. they are juicy and rich in vitamin C, flavonoids, potassium, and fiber.They can be eaten fresh, peeled, or squeezed to make juice.. They are mainly produced in the Mediterranean region and other warm climates.

What are the benefits of citrus fruits?

  • Being a good source of fiber

  • Reduced inflammation

  • Improved gastrointestinal function and health

  • Prevention of conditions like diabetes, cancer, and neurological disease

Some examples of citrus fruits

Some of the common citrus fruits are oranges, lemons, limes, mandarin, grapefruit, and pomelos.
